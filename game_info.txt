GAME_NAME = Farm On The Wheels

Locations:
1. Road(Main location)
2. Shops(Secondary location)

We can sell our stuff to some strangers.
How it works?
Player can purchase seeds, after this player can decide what he wants to grow
His car has its own limit of products(seeds)
Player can sell his items to strangers, and buy on this money more expensive(or not) seeds or upgrade his car or purchase soil.
